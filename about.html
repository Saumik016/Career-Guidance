About Career Compass

Career Compass is built with the vision of helping students, graduates, and professionals discover the right career path based on their interests and goals. We believe that the right information can change a person’s future, and we strive to offer clear, concise, and complete guidance across various career domains.

Our mission is to empower you with knowledge about what each career involves, the skills you need to succeed, and how to get started. Whether you are a student or a job switcher — we’ve got you covered!
